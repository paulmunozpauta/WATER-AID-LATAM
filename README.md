# WATER-AID-LATAM

Overview

This repository contains the datasets, figures, and supporting materials resulting from the needs analysis conducted during the preparation of the WATER-AID LATAM Erasmus+ CBHE Strand 2 proposal.

The needs analysis supports curriculum modernisation and institutional transformation in water-related master programmes across Chile, Ecuador, Peru, and partner institutions in Belgium and the Netherlands.

Scope of the dataset

The repository includes:

Aggregated results from a survey of 25 academic staff members across partner HEIs

Results of a curriculum mapping exercise covering more than 120 MSc-level courses

Summary figures on:

Courses prioritised for modernisation

Digital and AI tools identified for integration

Demand for new interdisciplinary courses

All data are anonymised and aggregated. No personal or sensitive information is included.

Methodology (summary)

The needs analysis combined:

Curriculum mapping of existing master programmes (course content, learning outcomes, digital integration)

Structured academic survey focusing on:

Modernisation priorities

Digital and AI skill needs

Labour-market alignment

Governance and employability gaps

Intended use

This dataset is intended for:

Curriculum development and reform projects

Comparative studies on digital transformation in higher education

Policy-oriented analysis of skills gaps in the water sector

Teaching and training purposes
